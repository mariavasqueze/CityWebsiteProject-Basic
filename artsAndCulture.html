Art & Culture

Paseo del Arte

A one-kilometre stretch that us home to three of the best museums in the world: the Prado, the Thyssen-Bornemisza, and the Reina Sofia.

Dance & Music

Enjoy performances of the most tipical dances and music from Spain. Flamenco is Spain’s most famous style of dance and music. During Franco’s dictatorship in the 60s, flamenco was adopted as one of the representative pillars of Spanish culture, but also used to oppose the regime that lasted till 1975. Enjoy a great night at the Corral de la Mojeria or Casa Patas.

Baroque Churches

Discover the 17th and 18th century Baroque style in the Churches of Madrid. The city has more than 200 churches. Enjoy the history and architecture with some of the most important buildings in the city. A great place to start is the Basilica of San Francis the Great, voted the most beautiful.
